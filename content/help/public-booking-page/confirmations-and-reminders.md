## Booking confirmation

Immediately after a client books, a confirmation is sent if:

- The client provided an **email address** — an HTML confirmation email is sent, OR
- The client **opted in to SMS** and provided a phone number — an SMS confirmation is sent

Both can be sent if the client has both channels and opted in. If a client provides no email and doesn't opt in to SMS, no confirmation is sent.

### What the confirmation email contains

The email is titled **"Your appointment is confirmed!"** and includes:

- Shop logo and name
- Client's name
- Barber, service, date, and time (start–end)
- Shop address, phone, and a "Book Again" link
- The shop's cancellation policy text (if one is enabled)
- If a guest was added, a separate "Guest Appointment" section

### SMS confirmation

If the client opted in, an SMS is sent immediately after booking with a short summary of the appointment (date, time, barber, and a link to manage the booking).

## 24-hour reminder

Stand Tall Booking automatically sends a **SMS reminder** approximately 24 hours before each appointment. The reminder is sent to clients who:

1. Opted in to SMS during booking
2. Have not already received a reminder for that appointment

The reminder window is 22–26 hours before the appointment start time. This means the exact timing may vary slightly — a 10:00 AM Tuesday appointment might get the reminder anywhere between 8:00 AM and 12:00 PM Monday.

**Email reminders are not sent.** Only SMS reminders are delivered.

## Cancellation policy

The shop can enable a custom cancellation policy in Settings → General. When enabled:

- The policy text appears at **Step 5 (Confirm Booking)** in a scrollable box
- A checkbox reading **"I agree to the cancellation policy"** must be checked before the "Confirm Booking" button becomes active
- The policy text is also included in the confirmation email

If the shop has not enabled a cancellation policy, Step 5 shows a simple "By confirming you agree to our cancellation policy." line instead of the full text and checkbox.

## Opting in to SMS

The SMS opt-in checkbox appears on the Confirm Booking step (Step 5) with this text:

> *"Yes, I agree to receive text messages from Stand Tall Booking, including appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates about my booking."*

Followed by:

> *"Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of booking."*

Opting in is entirely voluntary. A client can book without checking it.

## Cancellation notifications

When an appointment is cancelled — whether by the client from the portal or by the shop from the dashboard — a cancellation email is sent to the client if they have an email on file. The email states whether the deposit was refunded and includes a link to rebook.
