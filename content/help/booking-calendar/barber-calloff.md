When a barber calls off the day of their shift, you need to reach every client booked with them — fast. Stand Tall Booking's **Staff Call-Off Notifications** tool pulls up the full appointment list for a barber on a given day and lets you send them a message directly from the dashboard.

## Where to find it

**Settings → Call-Off**

This tab is visible to owners and managers.

## How to send call-off notifications

1. Go to **Settings → Call-Off**
2. Under **Select Staff Member**, choose the barber who called off from the dropdown
3. Under **Select Date**, click the date button to open a calendar picker and choose today's date (or whichever date is affected)
4. Stand Tall pulls up all **scheduled, confirmed, and checked-in** appointments for that barber on that date
5. Review the appointment list — each row shows the client name, appointment time, service, and email address
6. Use **Select All** to check every client, or individually check specific ones
7. Edit the **Message to Clients** — it's pre-filled with a template you can customize:

```
Hi [Client Name],

Unfortunately, [Barber Name] had to call off today due to illness.
We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience.

We'd be happy to reschedule your appointment. Please call us at
your earliest convenience.

Thank you for your understanding.

Stand Tall Barbershop
```

8. Click **Send Notifications to N Client(s)**

Notifications are sent via **email** to clients who have an email address on file. Clients without an email address on file won't receive an automated message — you'll need to reach them by phone.

## After sending

The selection is cleared and the message field resets. Clients receive their email and can follow up to reschedule.

To actually block the barber's schedule for the day (so no new online bookings come in), use the **Block Time** tool to add a block covering their full working hours, or turn off their online availability toggle temporarily under **Settings → Barbers**.

## Tips

- **Do this early.** The sooner clients know, the more likely they are to reschedule rather than walk in expecting their appointment.
- **Add a reschedule link.** The pre-filled template doesn't include a booking link — consider adding `standtallbooking.com/book/your-slug` to the message so clients can self-reschedule.
- **Not all clients have emails.** For clients with only a phone number on file, call them directly. The tool shows each client's email in the appointment list so you know who you still need to reach.
