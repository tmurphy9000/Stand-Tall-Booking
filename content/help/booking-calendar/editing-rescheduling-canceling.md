Once a booking is on the calendar you can update its status, move it to a new time or barber, resize it, or cancel it — all without leaving the calendar view.

## Opening the context menu

**Right-click** a booking card (or **long-press** on mobile) to open its context menu. The top of the menu shows the client's name, service, and booked time.

## Available actions

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **View Client** | Opens the client's profile page — booking history, spend, notes |
| **Confirm** | Marks the booking as confirmed (moves it from "Scheduled" to "Confirmed") |
| **Mark Arrived** | Marks the client as checked in |
| **Checkout** | Opens the Quick Checkout flow for this booking |
| **Mark as No-Show** | Records a no-show; increments the client's no-show count on their profile |
| **Mark as Late** | Records a late arrival; increments the client's late count |
| **Cancel** | Cancels the booking (see below) |

Actions only appear when they're valid for the current status. For example, **Confirm** only shows for bookings in "Scheduled" status.

## Canceling a booking

Clicking **Cancel** opens a confirmation dialog:

1. An optional **Reason** text field — fill it in if you want a record of why
2. **Keep** — dismisses without canceling
3. **Cancel Booking** — marks the booking as cancelled

If the client paid a deposit, the cancellation is processed through the refund system — the deposit is handled automatically based on your cancellation policy.

Cancelled bookings remain visible on the calendar (dimmed) so you have a record of the day.

## Rescheduling by drag-and-drop

To move a booking to a different time or barber:

1. Click and hold the booking card
2. Drag it to the target time slot (and optionally a different barber's column)
3. Release — the booking updates immediately with the new time, barber, and calculated end time

The booking's duration is preserved when you drag it. If you drag it to a barber's outside-hours slot, it will still move — there's no warning on drag.

## Resizing to change duration

To adjust how long a booking runs:

1. Hover over the **bottom edge** of a booking card — a resize handle appears
2. Drag down to make it longer, or up to shorten it
3. Release — the duration and end time update automatically

You can also drag the **top edge** to change the start time while keeping the end time fixed.

## What you can't change by editing

The calendar doesn't have a dedicated "edit all fields" modal for existing bookings. If you need to change the service, client name, or notes on a booking that's already been created, you can:

- Cancel it and create a new one with the correct details
- Use the client's profile page to view past booking records

Rescheduling (time, date, barber) is best done with drag-and-drop.
