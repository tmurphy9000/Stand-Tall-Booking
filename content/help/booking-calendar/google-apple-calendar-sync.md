> **Not yet available.** Google Calendar and Apple Calendar sync (iCal/CalDAV feed) is not currently built into Stand Tall Booking. This article explains what's available now and what's planned.

## Current status

Stand Tall Booking does not currently offer a Google Calendar sync, Apple Calendar sync, or an iCal/webcal feed. This means your bookings live in Stand Tall and aren't automatically reflected in a separate calendar app.

This feature is on the roadmap. When it ships, it will let each barber subscribe to their personal schedule as a read-only calendar feed they can add to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or any app that supports the iCal format.

## What you can do right now

**Bookmark the app on your phone.** The Stand Tall Booking web app works on any mobile browser. On iPhone, open it in Safari and tap **Share → Add to Home Screen**. On Android, use Chrome's **Add to Home Screen** option. It behaves like an installed app with one tap.

**Use the "My Schedule" mobile view.** On the Calendar, tap **My Schedule** (top of the grid on mobile) to see only your own column — no other barbers' appointments, no distractions. This is the closest equivalent to a personal calendar view within the app.

**Check the Time Off page.** Go to **Settings → Time Off** to submit time-off requests, see approved dates, and track your schedule around days off.

## When sync is available

Once Google/Apple Calendar sync is released, the setup will look something like this:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** (not yet available)
2. Find the **Calendar Sync** section
3. Copy your personal calendar feed URL
4. Open Google Calendar → **Other Calendars → From URL** and paste the link
5. The calendar updates automatically as bookings are created or changed

We'll update this article with exact steps when the feature ships.
