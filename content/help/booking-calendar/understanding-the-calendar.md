The Calendar is the main workspace in Stand Tall Booking. Everything you need for a day — who's in, what's booked, and what's open — is visible at a glance.

## Day view vs. week view

The calendar has two modes, selectable from the **Day / Week** pill buttons in the header:

| Mode | What you see |
|---|---|
| **Day** | Every barber's full column for a single date — the most detailed view for day-to-day operations |
| **Week** | A barber's week across seven days (Mon–Sun) — useful for spotting open days or patterns |

Switch between them by clicking **Day** or **Week** in the header bar. The view remembers the currently selected barber and date.

## Navigating dates

- **← / →** arrow buttons move backward or forward one day (or one week in Week view)
- **Today** button jumps back to the current date
- Click the **date label** in the center of the header (e.g. "Tuesday, Jul 8") to open a mini calendar popover — tap any date to jump directly to it

## Barber columns

In Day view, each active barber gets their own vertical column. If you have more than five barbers, they're grouped in pages of five — use the **< 1/2 >** page controls (top right of the grid) to scroll through groups.

Barbers who are off for the day (based on their schedule) are hidden from the column view by default. To see all barbers regardless of their schedule, go to **Settings → Display**.

## Mobile view

On a phone, the calendar shows a **My Schedule / All Barbers** toggle at the top of the grid:

- **My Schedule** — shows only the logged-in barber's column, filling the full width of the screen
- **All Barbers** — shows all active barbers, scrollable horizontally

## Reading the color legend

Booking cards are color-coded by status. Tap the **color swatch icon** (top-right of the header on desktop) to open the legend and see what each color means.

## Refreshing

If you're on a shared screen and another staff member just made a change, tap the **refresh icon** (circular arrow, top right) to pull in the latest data.
