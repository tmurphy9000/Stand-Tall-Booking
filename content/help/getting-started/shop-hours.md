Stand Tall Booking uses two layers of hours: **shop hours** (when your shop is generally open) and **barber hours** (the specific schedule for each barber). Understanding the difference helps avoid confusion with online availability.

## Shop hours vs. barber hours

| | Shop Hours | Barber Hours |
|---|---|---|
| **Where to set** | Settings → Shop Hours | Settings → Barbers → Edit → Hours |
| **What they control** | The overall open/closed days displayed on your public booking page | When a specific barber is available for bookings |
| **Who they apply to** | The entire shop | One individual barber |

In practice, **barber hours are what actually gate online bookings** — a client can only book a time slot if their chosen barber is marked as working that day and that time falls within that barber's hours. Shop hours provide the outer boundary.

## Setting your shop hours

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon at the bottom of the sidebar)
2. Click **Shop Hours** in the Settings sidebar
3. For each day of the week, use the toggle to mark the day **Open** or **Closed**
4. For open days, set the **Open** and **Close** times using the time fields
5. Click **Save Hours** in the top-right of the Shop Hours section

![Settings page showing the Barbers tab](/help-assets/screenshots/settings/settings.png)

> **Note:** After saving shop hours, return to the Setup Checklist and click **Mark as done ✓** on the "Set your shop hours" step — the checklist can't detect this change automatically.

## Setting per-barber hours

1. Go to **Settings → Barbers**
2. Click **Hours** on the barber whose schedule you want to set
3. Toggle each day on or off and set their start and end times
4. Save when done

Each barber's hours are independent, so you can reflect different schedules for different staff — for example, if one barber works Tuesday through Saturday while another works Monday through Friday.

## Common questions

**Why is a time slot greyed out on the calendar even though the shop is open?**
The barber assigned to that slot has different hours than the shop. Their individual schedule takes precedence.

**Can I block out a specific day for one barber without changing the shop hours?**
Yes — use the **Time-Off** feature (Settings → Time-Off) to mark a specific barber as unavailable on a particular date or date range without touching anyone else's schedule.

**What happens if I don't set barber hours?**
The barber won't show as available for any online bookings. Staff can still be booked manually from the admin calendar, but the public booking page won't show any availability for them.
