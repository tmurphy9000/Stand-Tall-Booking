Your public booking page is the first thing clients see when they tap your booking link. Stand Tall Booking lets you customize it with your logo, shop name, address, contact info, and social media profiles — all from **Settings → Booking Page**.

## Uploading your logo

Your logo appears at the top of the public booking page. It also appears in client-facing confirmation emails.

1. Go to **Settings → Booking Page**
2. Under **Shop Logo**, click **Upload Logo**
3. Select your file — PNG, JPG, or WebP accepted, max 2 MB
4. The logo previews immediately. Click **Save** in the top-right to apply

**Recommended dimensions:** 200×200 px. Square logos work best since the booking page displays them in a square container.

If you don't upload a logo, the booking page shows a default placeholder.

## Shop name and address

These appear on the booking page so clients know exactly where to go.

1. Under **Shop Info**, fill in:
   - **Shop Name** — your barbershop's public-facing name
   - **Address** — your street address, city, state, and ZIP
   - **Business Phone** (optional) — shown on the booking page; leave blank to hide
   - **Business Email** (optional) — shown on the booking page; leave blank to hide
2. Click **Save**

## Social media links

Add links to your Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok profiles. When enabled, these appear as icons on the booking page welcome screen — clients can tap them to visit your social profiles before they book.

1. Under **Social Media**, find the platform you want to add
2. Toggle the switch next to it to **on**
3. Paste your full profile URL in the text field (e.g. `https://instagram.com/yourshop`)
4. Repeat for any other platforms
5. Click **Save**

> **Setup Checklist note:** After saving social links, go back to the checklist and click **Mark as done ✓** on the "Add your social media links" step — the checklist doesn't detect this change automatically.

Platforms available: **Instagram**, **Facebook**, **TikTok**

## Cancellation policy

You can display a cancellation policy that clients must acknowledge before completing a booking.

1. Under **Cancellation Policy**, toggle it **on**
2. Write your policy text in the text area — e.g. "We require at least 24 hours notice. Late cancellations may result in a charge."
3. Click **Save**

When enabled, clients see this text on the confirmation step and must check a box to confirm they've read it before the booking goes through.

## Previewing your booking page

To see how your booking page looks to clients:

1. In **Settings → Booking Page**, find the **Your Booking URL** section
2. Click **Open** to view it in a new tab

Any changes you save in settings are reflected on the live booking page immediately.
