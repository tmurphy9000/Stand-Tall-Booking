Services define what clients can book and what you charge. Each service has a name, duration, price, and optional category. These are what clients see when they book online, and what drives your checkout and payroll calculations.

## Adding a service

1. Go to **Settings → Services**
2. Click the **Add** button in the top-right of the Services section
3. Fill in the service details:

| Field | Options / Notes |
|---|---|
| **Name** | e.g. Haircut, Skin Fade, Beard Trim |
| **Duration** | 15, 30, 45, 60, 75, 90, or 120 minutes |
| **Price ($)** | The amount charged to the client |
| **Category** | Optional grouping label — e.g. "Haircut", "Beard", "Kids" |

4. Click **Create**

The service immediately appears in the booking flow for clients, and in the service selector when creating bookings from the admin calendar.

## Editing or deleting a service

- Click the **pencil icon** on any service row to edit its name, duration, price, or category
- Click the **trash icon** to delete it — this cannot be undone, so make sure no upcoming bookings depend on it before deleting

## Categories

Categories are free-text labels you assign to services. They don't change how booking works — they're organizational. If you add a category like "Beard" to beard-related services, it makes your service list easier to read in the dashboard, but clients see a flat list on the booking page.

## Commission settings

By default, each barber earns a percentage of every service based on their **Service Commission Rate** (set on their barber record). If you need a service-specific override — for example, if a premium service has a different commission structure — use the per-barber **Services** button (Settings → Barbers → Services) to configure custom durations per barber, not per service.

Commission rates for each barber are set under **Settings → Barbers → Edit**. See [Adding Barbers & Staff](/help/getting-started/adding-barbers) for details.

## Per-barber service durations

Some barbers are faster or slower at certain services. You can configure different durations for a specific barber:

1. Go to **Settings → Barbers**
2. Click **Services** on the barber whose durations you want to customize
3. Set a custom duration for any service — leave others at the shop default

This means if a senior barber does a skin fade in 25 minutes but it books as 30 by default, you can keep the system-wide default at 30 and override it to 25 for that specific barber. This affects how the calendar blocks time, but not the price charged to the client.
